What is this site?

This is a static copy of a php/MySQL site that I built and used to track movies and TV shows that I watched. The site was heavily centered around the Browser Extension that connected viewing history to IMDb.

I built the earliest form of the site in 2007 when I was supposed working on other things (details in the entry on my old project site). I had integrated the site with Trakt.tv, including migrating all past data, so eventually in 2019 I moved over to Trakt completely.
